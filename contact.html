Feedback about your driver

You may also wish to send feedback directly to your local Licencing Department for incidents related to your driver. When contacting your local authority, remember to include:

  • A photo/note of the driver number​
  • the vehicle registration number
  • the license plate number
  • the date, time, and where the incident happened.
